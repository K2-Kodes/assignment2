Yuta

Yuta Okkotsu is a young man with black hair and dark blue eyes.he has dark circles under his eyelids from a lack of sleep. Copy innate technique that allows him to replicate another sorcerer's innate technique. Rika is a cursed spirit that remains with Yuta after Rika Orimoto passed away.

What is Copy? While connected to Rika, Yuta can copy innate techniques and use them. He can even use multiple different techniques that he's copied in quick succession. The conditions under which he can copy a technique is: He has to have Rika eat a part of a sorcerer's body to gain a copy of a technique. The more body parts Rika is stronger and easier it is to copy the technique, as it is easier to analyse the cursed energy. For more complex cursed techniques, training is still required to use them.

What is Rika?The cursed spirit that remains with Yuta after Rika Orimoto passed away. Most of her body is made up of a black mass but she also has two muscular human-like arms. She serves as the external storage for Yuta's cursed techniques and cursed tools.
